## Source Notes

- IFAB is the controlling source for the Laws and should outrank pundit commentary, Wikipedia, and league-specific custom unless the task is explicitly about a particular competition.
- "Spirit of the game" is not a loophole for vibes. Use it to preserve fairness, safety, and playable continuity inside the Laws.
- VAR's clear-and-obvious standard is a governance design principle, not a promise that review makes decisions controversy-free.
- Memoirs and interviews from Collina/Webb are lineage and craft evidence, not binding law. Use them for preparation, presence, and pressure, not to override IFAB sources.
- Elite professional refereeing sources can overfit to televised matches with crews, VAR, and communications infrastructure. For grassroots analogies, emphasize common sense, safety, and reporting.
