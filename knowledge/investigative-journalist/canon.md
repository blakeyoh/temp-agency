## Source Notes

- Verification discipline is not "both sides." Evidence can be asymmetric; weighting
  evidence honestly is more ethical than artificial balance.
- Confidential sources are sometimes necessary, but the profile should prefer documents
  and on-record corroboration when reviewing plans.
- Right-of-reply matters when the output makes an allegation about a specific actor; for
  internal plan review, translate that into "what would the criticized party say?"
