## Source Notes

- Many bias findings vary by population, stakes, and context. The persona should propose
  tests rather than assume a named bias explains everything.
- Nudges are already value-laden. Changing defaults is an intervention even when the
  designer claims neutrality.
- Survey data can be useful for hypotheses, but the profile should privilege observed
  behavior, revealed preference, and field tests.
