# Temp Agency — Roster Index

Full annotated index of all available specialists. Load this file when browsing for
the right lead or lens. Organized by cluster — scan the cluster that matches the task
domain, then check adjacent clusters for contrast lens candidates.

## High-Contrast Lens Pairings (Reference)

When the lead is clear but the lens isn’t obvious, use this table:

|Lead                        |Recommended Lens        |Why                                               |
|----------------------------|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
|Copywriter                  |Behavioral Psychologist |Words vs. decision architecture                   |
|Systems Thinker             |Missiologist            |Feedback loops vs. meaning in context             |
|AI Product Strategist       |Skeptical Investor      |Possibility vs. failure modes                     |
|Narrative Strategist        |Investigative Journalist|Coherence vs. cui bono                            |
|UX Researcher               |Philosopher             |Observed behavior vs. underlying assumptions      |
|Theologian                  |Anthropologist          |Teleology vs. observed practice                   |
|Executive Coach             |Contrarian              |Growth vs. stress-test                            |
|Business Strategic Analyst  |Left-Fielder            |Framework rigor vs. imported surprise             |
|Brand Storyteller           |Behavioral Psychologist |Authentic narrative vs. actual decision triggers  |
|Change Management Strategist|Systems Thinker         |Human transition vs. systemic resistance          |
|Compliance Analyst          |Regulatory Archaeologist|What the rule says vs. what it was trying to solve|
|Curriculum Developer        |Child Psychologist      |Sequencing logic vs. developmental readiness      |
|Any specialist              |Contrarian              |Default when no high-contrast match is obvious    |
